Gloucester Adventures

Experience Gloucester’s Charm

Set beside the scenic Robinswood Hill Country Park and close to Gloucester Golf Club, Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday offers the perfect base to explore both the peaceful countryside and the rich history of Gloucester city centre. From the magnificent Gloucester Cathedral to the vibrant Gloucester Docks, discover a perfect mix of heritage, culture, and outdoor adventures.

Local Attractions

Discover Gloucester

Gloucester Cathedral

One of Britain’s most beautiful cathedrals, famous for its architecture and as a filming location for Harry Potter. Just a short drive from the hotel.

Gloucester Docks

A historic waterfront area with warehouses, shops, restaurants, and museums. A vibrant spot for walks and dining.

Museum of Gloucester

Explore the city’s rich history through fascinating exhibits and collections, located in the heart of Gloucester.

Robinswood Hill Country Park

Right on the hotel’s doorstep – enjoy scenic walks, views over Gloucester, and outdoor activities in this beautiful country park.
