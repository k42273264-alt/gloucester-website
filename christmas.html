Festive Celebrations

Christmas at Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday

Set beside scenic Robinswood Hill Country Park, Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday offers a relaxed countryside setting just minutes from Gloucester city centre. With 95 bedrooms, a restaurant and bar, four flexible event spaces, leisure and spa facilities, and convenient access from the M5, the hotel is well suited for festive gatherings, seasonal dining and overnight celebrations.

Festive Options

Plan Your Seasonal Celebration

Festive Parties

Our four flexible event spaces make the hotel ideal for festive social celebrations, seasonal gatherings and end-of-year events. Merlot is the largest and most adaptable suite, with a private bar and the option to divide into Merlot 1 and Merlot 2.

Perfect for corporate celebrations, festive dinners and larger group events in a relaxed hotel setting.

Private Festive Events

Robinswood is well suited to medium-sized private events, festive lunches and social dining, while Syndicate 1 and Syndicate 2 are ideal for smaller gatherings, planning meetings or intimate private celebrations.

Our team can help you create a seasonal event that fits your group size and preferred atmosphere.

Festive Stays & Dining

Make your celebration even easier with 95 guest bedrooms, on-site restaurant and bar, complimentary Wi-Fi, leisure club access, spa facilities and parking available at £5 per 24 hours.

Guests can enjoy a comfortable festive stay with check-in from 3:00pm and check-out at 11:00am.
