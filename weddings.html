Your Special Day

Weddings at Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday

Set beside Robinswood Hill, our hotel offers a relaxed and elegant setting for weddings, civil ceremonies, receptions and celebrations. With dedicated co-ordination, tailored drinks and dining options, 95 bedrooms for your guests, and flexible event spaces, we help create a memorable day designed around your vision.

Food & Drink

Wedding Dining Options

Wedding Breakfast

Select one starter, one main and one dessert for your guests from a chef-prepared range of menu options. Vegetarian and dietary requirements can be accommodated.

Menu Tasting

Complimentary menu tasting for two is included within your package, with additional guests available at an extra fee.

Kids Menus

A dedicated children’s package includes a three course meal and a soft drink, with vegetarian and dietary requirements catered for.

Evening Buffet

Buffet options are available for evening celebrations, and your wedding cake can also be cut and served as part of the evening buffet if you wish.
