Enjoy Great Food & Drink

Our on-site restaurant and bar offer a relaxed setting with tasty food and friendly service. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or a casual drink after exploring Gloucester or Robinswood Hill Country Park.

Our Restaurant

Guests can enjoy a range of thoughtful services designed to ensure a comfortable and convenient stay. The hotel operates a 24-hour reception with check-in from 3:00pm and check-out at 11:00am. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged at an additional cost. Laundry services are available on request, and selected packages include a welcome drink on arrival.

On-site parking is available at £5.00 for 24 hours, and the property is monitored by CCTV for added security.
