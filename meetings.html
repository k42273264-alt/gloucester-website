Meetings & Events

Professional Events in Gloucester

Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday offers four flexible, purpose-built meeting spaces for up to 180 delegates. Ideal for conferences, banquets, corporate dinners, private events and breakout sessions, the hotel combines dedicated event support with 95 bedrooms, a brasserie, lounge and bar, plus leisure facilities including a pool, gym, spa, steam room and sauna.

Our Venues

Dedicated Meeting Rooms

Merlot Suite

Our largest event suite, accommodating up to 180 delegates theatre style. Merlot is ideal for large conferences and events, with partition walls for flexible layouts, a private reception area, bar, washroom facilities, and direct access to the patio and gardens.

Max theatre: 180 Boardroom: 60 Cabaret: 120

Robinswood

A great meeting room close to reception, featuring natural daylight and a 50-inch flat screen TV. Ideal for meetings and stylish private dining, with space for up to 50 delegates theatre style.

Max theatre: 50 Boardroom: 16 Dinner: 40

Syndicate 1

A compact syndicate room ideal for interviews, conference calls and breakout sessions. Best suited to smaller business meetings in a private setting.

Boardroom: 6 Length: 3.90m Width: 3.40m

Syndicate 2

Ideal for small boardroom meetings, interviews and breakout use. Slightly larger than Syndicate 1, it provides a practical private space for up to 8 delegates boardroom style.

Boardroom: 8 Length: 5.60m Width: 3.40m
