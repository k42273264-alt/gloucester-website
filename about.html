Our Story

Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday

A Countryside Haven

Nestled beside the scenic Robinswood Hill Country Park, Gloucester Robinswood Hotel by Sunday offers a perfect blend of contemporary luxury and peaceful surroundings. Just minutes from Gloucester city centre, this 95-bedroom hotel is ideal for leisure, business, weddings, and special events.

Modern Comfort & Facilities

Enjoy extensive leisure facilities including a swimming pool, spa with 4 treatment rooms, sauna, steam room, and a fully equipped gym. The hotel also features a restaurant & bar, 4 flexible event spaces, complimentary Wi-Fi, and on-site parking (£5 per 24 hours).

Location

Beside Robinswood Hill

Set in a peaceful countryside location next to Robinswood Hill Country Park and close to Gloucester Golf Club, yet only minutes from Gloucester city centre. Perfect for both relaxation and easy access to local attractions.

📍 Nearby Attractions:

  • Gloucester Cathedral – Historic landmark
  • Gloucester Docks – Vibrant waterfront area
  • Museum of Gloucester – Rich local history
  • Robinswood Hill Country Park – Right on the doorstep
  • Gloucester Golf Club – Adjacent to the hotel
  • Nature in Art – 3 miles away
  • Gloucester Ski & Snowboard Centre

🌟 Key Benefits of the Location

  • ✅ Ideal base for leisure and family stays
  • ✅ Great for weddings and corporate events

Getting Here

Arrive Easily

Nearest station: Gloucester Station – 3 miles (accessible via Number 10 bus every 10 minutes).

Number 10 bus stops at “Matson Shops” – just a 5-minute walk from the hotel.

From M5:

Exit at Junction 11A or Junction 12 and follow signs towards Robinswood Hill / Gloucester Golf Club.

On-site parking available at £5 per 24 hours.

Gloucestershire Airport – 8.7 miles
Bristol International Airport – 45.3 miles
